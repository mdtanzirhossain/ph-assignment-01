Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Our scientists have unique and innovative mindset. They need proper environment to flourish.

Sustainability

We need updated policy to achieve economic growth while reducing environmental impact.

Infrastructure

To improve the infrastructure, we can focus on developing a strong railway network.

Empowerment

To increase large number of unemployed people, we need to provide life skills education.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

We need to adopt and integrate of advanced digital technologies across all aspects of society, including government, businesses, and individuals, often leveraging technologies like artificial intelligence and cloud computing.

Green Energy

We should utilize green energy by investing in renewable sources like solar, wind and hydro power to provide reliable, clean electricity access, mitigating climate change impacts while promoting economic growth by creating jobs.

Modern Infrastructure

It is high time, we adopted modern infrastructure prioritizing strategic planning, leveraging public-private partnerships, investing in sustainable technologies, seeking international aid to optimize operations.
